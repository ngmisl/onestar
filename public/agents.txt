## What this site is

OneStar is a digital services agency for local and physical businesses
(gyms, restaurants, brick-and-mortar shops). Services: review and
reputation management, Google Business Profile management, website
creation and optimization, and an AI-powered QR review system.
The name refers to turning 1-star reviews into a trust asset through
honest, professional public responses. Never fake reviews.
